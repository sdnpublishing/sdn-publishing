About SDN Publishing

Ideas worth reading.
Readers worth reaching.

SDN Publishing is an independent non-fiction publisher committed to one principle: that rigorous ideas, communicated with clarity and care, can change the way people see the world.

Our catalogue spans twelve subject areas — from the hard sciences and ancient history to spiritual traditions, financial literacy, psychological insight, and the creative arts. We do not believe in silos. The most interesting thinking happens at the edges of disciplines, and our authors reflect that conviction.

Every book we publish is written for a general reader — someone curious, intelligent, and unwilling to be talked down to. We hold our authors to high standards of research and high standards of prose. We believe a book should earn your time.

SDN Publishing operates as a fully independent publisher with global distribution through Amazon KDP. Our authors retain creative control and publish under their own names across our network of subject-specialist imprints.

Submission & Enquiries

We are currently closed to unsolicited submissions. For rights enquiries, media requests, or bulk orders, please contact us at publishing@sdnpublishing.com.
